Optimal Massage

Building a stronger local presence and better website

The Problem

A massage therapist with strong client reviews and a loyal base, but an inconsistent online presence holding back growth. The website was costly, outdated, and not optimised for local search — limiting new client acquisition and making it harder for people to find and trust the business online.

What I Did

Conducted a full audit of the Google Business profile and existing website. Designed and built a newer, cheaper-to-run website with improved branding, imagery, and UX. Optimised for local SEO — including crafting a focused business description, selecting the right categories, and positioning the therapist's multilingual offer (English, Italian, Spanish) as a competitive differentiator.

Key Improvements

Brand consistency

Unified the business name and messaging across all platforms — website, Google Business, and social — to avoid confusion and build recognition with new visitors.

Google Business optimisation

Rewrote the business description with relevant keywords, added primary and secondary service categories, and highlighted multilingual capabilities to broaden reach across English, Italian, and Spanish speakers.

Website redesign

Built a cleaner, faster site with better imagery and a clear service hierarchy — making it easier for visitors to understand the offer and book, at a fraction of the running cost.

Local SEO

Optimised for searches in both Mallorca and London, with location-specific language and a strong call-to-action throughout to convert local search intent into enquiries.

Outcome

Increased inbound enquiries and faster response from prospective clients — contributing to a measurable uplift in monthly revenue. The new site costs significantly less to run while performing better across search and conversion.
